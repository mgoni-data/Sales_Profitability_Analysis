# Key Findings – Sales & Profitability Analysis

This analysis explored regional, category, and product-level profitability using SQL and Tableau.

  # 1. Regional Performance
      > West and East regions recorded the highest profitability.
      > Central region showed the lowest profit performance.
      > South region performed moderately compared to other regions.

  # 2. Category Performance
      > Technology was the most profitable category across all regions.
      > Office Supplies showed stable and consistent performance.
      > Furniture underperformed in the Central region.

  # 3. Central Region Issue
      > Furniture in the Central region recorded negative profit.
      > This indicates inefficiencies in pricing or cost structure within that category.

  # 4. Product-Level Losses
      > A small group of office equipment products consistently generated losses.
      > Examples include printers, binding machines, and office accessories.

  # 5. Discount Impact
      > A negative relationship was observed between discount and profit.
      > Higher discounts generally resulted in lower profitability.

  # 6. Final Insight
       > The main profitability issue is not demand but pricing efficiency.
      > Losses are concentrated in Furniture (Central region) and specific product categories.





  
- The main profitability issue is not demand but pricing efficiency.
- Losses are concentrated in Furniture (Central region) and specific product categories.
